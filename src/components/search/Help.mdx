<h3 style="margin: 0;">What is this?</h3>

This is ilo Muni pi mute nimi, "word-frequency tool named Muni." It's a graphing
tool like [Google Ngrams](https://books.google.com/ngrams/), but for [Toki
Pona](https://tokipona.org), a constructed language with 120-130 words.
If you don't know what to search, press <Icon name="mingcute:refresh-3-line" /> to
make a random query!

<h3 style="line-height: 0.5em;">Search</h3>

- Search **1 to 6 word phrases**: "toki pona"
- Search **multiple phrases** with commas: "soweli, waso"
- Use a **wildcard** `*` to get top matching phrases: "ona li \*"
- **Add** phrases with `+`: "ale + ali"
- **Subtract** phrases with `-`: "toki - toki pona"
- Limit **minimum sentence length** with `_1-6` on a phrase: "toki_2, toki_3"

<h3 style="margin-bottom: 0; line-height: 0.5em;">Options</h3>

- **Relative** shows what % of all words are the searched word
- **Absolute** shows how many times the searched word appeared
- **Smoothing** averages out noisy graphs, but too much can be misleading
- The rest is complicated! [See the help page for more.](/ilo-muni/help)
